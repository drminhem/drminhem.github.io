Evidence-Based Cancer Care with Clear Communication

My practice emphasizes transparent discussions, practical next steps, and careful alignment with major guidelines (e.g., NCCN/ESMO/ASCO) — while tailoring decisions to the individual patient’s goals, risks, and clinical context.

Clinical focus

Adult hematology & oncology, including solid tumors and blood disorders — with a structured approach to diagnosis, treatment planning, and follow-up.

How decisions are made

Shared decision-making based on current evidence, multidisciplinary input when needed, and a clear explanation of benefits, risks, and alternatives.

Philosophy & Pillars of Care

Three pillars guide my daily practice: precision oncology, modern evidence-based care, and compassionate support.

Precision Oncology

DNA testing and actionable biomarkers to inform targeted therapy, immunotherapy, and individualized treatment strategies.

Modern Evidence-Based Care

Up-to-date practice anchored in American and European guidelines, multidisciplinary discussion, and outcomes-driven care.

Compassionate & Supportive Care

Human-centered care that prioritizes communication, symptom relief, supportive and palliative principles, and continuity.
