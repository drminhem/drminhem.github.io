About

Evidence-Based Cancer Care with Clear Communication

My approach combines up-to-date guidelines, practical decision-making, and supportive care — with attention to each patient’s goals, values, and quality of life.

Clinical Focus

Adult hematology and oncology care including solid tumors and hematologic malignancies, as well as complex blood disorders. Care is grounded in contemporary evidence and multidisciplinary collaboration.

Guideline-Based Practice

Decisions are guided by major oncology standards and peer-reviewed evidence — with a patient-centered discussion of benefits, risks, and alternatives.

Philosophy

Three Pillars of Care

A modern oncology practice requires precision, evidence, and compassion — delivered consistently and clearly.

01

Precision Medicine

Integrating actionable genomic information and modern systemic therapy — including targeted therapy and immunotherapy — to personalize treatment when appropriate.

02

Modern Evidence-Based Care

Applying the latest high-quality evidence, multidisciplinary input, and international guidelines to support safe, high-standard decisions.

03

Compassionate Supportive Care

Clear communication, symptom control, and supportive/palliative-focused care principles — aiming to reduce suffering and improve quality of life.
